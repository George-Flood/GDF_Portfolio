Nuance VIP Catalogue.

Designed for the VIP lounge of Sochi airport as a solution to the problem of VIP passengers bypassing the main store in the airport, this catalogue needed to be sophisticated but not alienating. The vibrant gradient cover assist in helping it stand out from the array of competing literature, whereas the use of thin typography helps give the catalogue an elegant edge. The minimal layout and extensive use of whitespace aid in pulling focus on to the products and the introduction of block colours and category specific quotes insure that the catalogue is easy to navigate.
